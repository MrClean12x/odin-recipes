Brown Chicken

Description

Lazy fried chicken made using breadcrumbs, appropriately named by a 4 year old

  • chicken tenderloins
  • italian breadcrumbs
  • cooking oil
  • milk

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in large deep pan (enough to submerge the chicken), medium/high heat
  2. Dip chicken in milk then roll around in breadcrumbs until fully covered
  3. Place chicken in oil, cook one side for 5 minutes then flip
  4. Remove chicken and place on plate to cool after browning both sides
  5. enjoy!
