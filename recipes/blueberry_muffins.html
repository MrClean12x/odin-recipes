Blueberry Muffins

Description

Blueberry muffin recipe, yay

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/3 cup canola oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/3 to 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • about 1 cup fresh or frozen blueberries

Steps:

  1. throw that shit together
  2. put it in the muffin tray
  3. pre heat that oven yo
  4. throw it in the oven
  5. take it out
  6. enjoy!
