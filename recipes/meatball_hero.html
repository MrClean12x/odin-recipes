Meatball Hero

>

Description

Classic Italian meatball recipe with cheese on toasted bread

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • 2 eggs
  • oregano
  • garlic powder
  • 1 cup italian breadcrumbs
  • grated parmesan
  • Pasta sauce, enough to fully submerge meatballs in pan, 1 or 2 jars
  • grated mozzarella cheese
  • italian loaf or french baguette

Steps:

  1. Combine all ingredients, except the pasta sauce, mozzarella cheese, and bread, in a mixing bowl and mix together with your hands
  2. Spray a large, deep pan with oil, set heat to medium
  3. Wait a minute or two for the heat to oil and, make the mixture from mixing bowl into balls, brown each side
  4. Pour in enough pasta sauce to fully submerge the meatballs
  5. Simmer on low heat for 1 hour, stirring every 10-15 minutes, keep covered
  6. Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees F
  7. After simmering for an hour place meatballs on bread sliced open to make a sandwich
  8. Add pasta sauce from pan, and top with mozzarella
  9. Place in oven for 10-15 to toast the bread and melt the cheese
  10. enjoy!
